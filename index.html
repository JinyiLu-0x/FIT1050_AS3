Nosham University Badminton Club brings together students and community players, and welcomes participants of all skill levels.

Weekly Social Sessions

Regular Friday and Sunday sessions help students form a routine and make it easy to join.

Beginner-Friendly Culture

New players can join without needing advanced skills, because the club focuses on friendly games and peer support.

Equipment Support

We provide rackets and shared shuttlecocks to make first-time visitors feel at ease, especially students who want to experience the club first.

Community Events

Relaxing events, photo moments and team gatherings - all these can make the club feel more friendly and welcoming.
